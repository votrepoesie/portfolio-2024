Overview ✨

In this class project, I received a (fake) design brief from Bumble and built upon the existing Google Material Design system to address user needs derived from a business goal. The project focused on creating a design intervention that facilitates organic connections with people you pass by, enhancing the user experience by bridging digital interactions with real-world encounters.

Discovering User Needs

After understanding the business goal, the next step was to explore how geolocation can create value for users. Due to time constraint, I conducted research through Reddit and other online forums by collecting various complaints from Bumble users in the BFF and BIZZ categories. From there, I created 3 user personas and came up with solutions for their problems with the 5W1H method.

It's important to keep in mind that users are not interested in knowing other people’s location; they’re interested in the service that it provides.
